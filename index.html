        About

        Hello, Welcome to CAMPSITE.

        Campsite is a pet project, and was developed to create a space where ideas and concepts can be brought to life. It is a place for learning, experimenting, and most importantly... fun!

        Similar to how stories and ideas and usually shared huddled over a campfire, Campsite is a "site" where such ideas can be created and implemented.
